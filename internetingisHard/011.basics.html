Optional Trailing Slash

It doesn’t really make a difference whether you use or not trailing slash, but pick one and stick to it for the sake of consistency. You should include the trailing "/" character because it clearly shows that it’s a self-closing element. This will help prevent your eyes from searching for the closing tag elsewhere in the document.
